What is clide?

Clide has initially been developed as a proof of concept of a web interface for the interactive thorem proover Isabelle. Currently it is extended into a language agnostic, collaborative IDE running completely in the cloud at your service.
Clide is maintained by Martin Ring at the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI) in Bremen, Germany.
If you want to know what is running under the hood, feel free to explore the github project.
