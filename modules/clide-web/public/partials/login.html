What is this?

Clide is a language agnostic, collaborative IDE running in the cloud at your service.

It is developed and maintained by Martin Ring at the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI) in Bremen, Germany.

If you want to know what is running under the hood or you want to build a plugin, feel free to explore the github project or get in touch.
